Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest tech conferences in the world, bringing together innovators, developers, and leaders in the industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace: Pioneer in computational algorithms and a celebrated speaker at TechCon 2015.
  • Mr. Alan Turing: Renowned for his contributions to AI, Mr. Turing inspired attendees at TechCon 2018.
  • Ms. Grace Hopper: A visionary in programming languages who captivated the audience at TechCon 2020.
